Manual Tester
Location: Noida (Currently Remote)
Apply Now

We are looking for an energetic, highly passionate software quality expert to join our team. Someone who is excited to join a fast growing software company is most welcome. The ideal candidate should have a "No-compromise" attitude towards quality. As a software tester, he/she should have a crystal clear understanding of what a good quality software means from a customer's perspective.

"Must" be ready to join under 30 days. Lucrative bonus for early joiners.

Technical Skills
  • Manual testing experience of 3-5 years of industry experience .
  • Very good knowledge of Test preparation and execution
  • In-depth knowledge of web applications
  • Well versed with Web APIs
  • Expert in SoapUI or POSTMAN.
  • Very good knowledge of Relational databases.
  • Knowledge of JIRA is must
  • Knowledge of CI/CD pipelines is a plus.
  • Must have a basic knowledge of frequently used AWS services like EC2, RDS etc.
Must have attributes
  • Excellent communication skills
  • Confident enough to directly interact with clients
  • Really good at problem solving
  • Very good analytical skills
  • A strong team player with a 'Team First' attitude.
What we have got for you
  • Highly competitive Salary packages
  • An opportunity to play the crucial role in a fast growing company.
  • Clear & well defined career paths.
  • An opportunity to work with a team who is serious enough about their clients success, but at the same time is equally fun loving and enthusiastic about the way it works.

If you think the above points excite you, then please share your resume at careers@brainstacktechnologies.com. We would love to interact with you and understand how can we add value to each others growth story.

At Brainstack Technologies, we deliver complete range of software services starting from ideation to execution, thus enabling our global clients to outperform the competition.
